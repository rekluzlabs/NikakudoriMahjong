Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

Nikakudori Mahjong by Rekluz labs

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nikakudori Mahjong ("the Game"). At Rekluz labs, we're committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application on Google Play Store.

We believe in transparency and simplicity. Nikakudori Mahjong is designed to be a lightweight, privacy-respecting game that doesn't require extensive data collection.

2. What Information We Collect

Nikakudori Mahjong collects minimal personal information. Here's what we gather:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Feedback & Support: If you contact us via in-app feedback form, we may collect your message and optional contact information (email) so we can respond to you.

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Android device model, OS version, and app version for crash reporting and debugging.
  • Gameplay Analytics: Non-identifying gameplay data (game mode played, board layouts attempted, session length) to improve game design and difficulty balance. This data is anonymized and not linked to your identity.
  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, we automatically collect crash logs to fix bugs and improve stability.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or personal identity (unless you voluntarily provide it in feedback)
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or calendar information
  • Your browsing history outside the app
  • Payment information (the game is free with no in-app purchases)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Game Improvement: Gameplay analytics help us understand which board layouts are fun, what difficulty levels work, and where players struggle, so we can improve the game.
  • Bug Fixing: Crash reports help us identify and fix technical issues.
  • Customer Support: If you submit feedback, we use it to respond to your questions or suggestions.
  • Legal Compliance: We may process information as required by law or to protect against fraud.

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or selling to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data security is important to us. Here's how we protect it:

  • Gameplay analytics and crash reports are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.
  • We retain gameplay analytics for a reasonable period to track game performance, then delete old data.
  • Feedback submissions are retained to respond to your inquiry, then may be deleted at your request.

Note: While we take security seriously, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please report any security concerns to the contact email below.

5. Third-Party Services

Nikakudori Mahjong may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services: For crash reporting and app analytics. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Feedback Forms (Tally.so or Google Forms): If you submit feedback via our in-app form, your submission is sent to these services. Review their privacy policies for details.

We do not share your personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: You can request what data we have about you.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your data (e.g., feedback submissions or associated analytics).
  • Opt-Out: You can disable crash reporting and analytics in the app settings.
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

Nikakudori Mahjong is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we've collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to us can contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy and, if necessary, through the app or our website.

Your continued use of Nikakudori Mahjong after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Gameplay Analytics: Retained for 90 days, then aggregated and anonymized.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 30 days for debugging purposes.
  • Feedback & Support: Retained until your inquiry is resolved, then deleted unless you ask us to keep it.
